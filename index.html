Mutual Crypto Wallet Agreement
This Mutual Crypto Wallet Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into by and between the undersigned parties, collectively referred to as "All Party Members."

1. Purpose of the Wallet:

All Party Members agree to establish a mutual crypto wallet for the purpose of making investments and managing digital assets collectively.

2. Contributions:

2.1. All Party Members commit to contributing either on a weekly or monthly basis, as specified below:

Weekly Monthly

2.2. Minimum Contribution: The minimum contribution for those investing on a weekly basis is $10, while for those investing on a monthly basis, it is $40.

2.3. Early contributions are allowed and encouraged.

2.4. Late Payment: In the event an All Party Member fails to make a timely contribution, they shall be charged an additional $5 on the following contribution, which includes the amount they were short on or the late payment. Failure to make the late payment shall result in an additional $5 penalty.

3. Investment Decisions:

3.1. Any individual wishing to propose an investment or financial action shall do so, and such proposals shall be voted upon by All Party Members.

3.2. Investment approval shall require a majority vote of All Party Members.

3.3. In case an investment proposal is rejected by the majority, the investment shall be disregarded until a new request is made.

4. Investment Requests:

4.1. Each individual is allowed one investment request per day, with flexibility in the case of unusual events or new opportunities.

4.2. Any individual recognizes that they have the option to invest more at any time. However, it is agreed that when the majority party agrees to withdraw a certain profit amount, the profits will be split equally among all party members, regardless of the individual contribution amounts, as long as the minimum investment requirements are met.

5. Withdrawals:

5.1. In no case can an individual withdraw their funds from the mutual wallet unless there is a financial emergency, and unless the majority party agrees to the withdrawal.

6. Acknowledgment of Risks:

6.1. Each All Party Member acknowledges and recognizes the risks associated with participating in this mutual crypto wallet. By signing this Agreement, each member affirms their willingness to contribute to the group and their understanding of the potential risks involved.

7. Signatures:

By signing this Agreement, each All Party Member acknowledges their understanding of and agreement to the terms and conditions outlined herein.
